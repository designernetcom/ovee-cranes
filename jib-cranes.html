Jib Cranes

A jib crane is a type of lifting device that uses a horizontal arm (jib or boom) to support a moveable hoist. It's a versatile material handling solution that is commonly used in industrial settings for tasks such as lifting and moving loads within a specific radius. Jib cranes come in various configurations, with the two primary types being free-standing (floor-mounted) and wall-mounted.

Superior durability and dependability are ensured by the use of premium materials and state-of-the-art technologies in the manufacturing of our jib cranes. Since every customer has different needs, we provide a large range of jib cranes, including floor-mounted, wall-mounted, and portable models. We offer the ideal option for you whether you require a jib crane for heavy-duty industrial applications or light-duty lifting.
